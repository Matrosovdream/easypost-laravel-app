# Data Processing Addendum

**Effective date:** April 1, 2026

This is a placeholder. Replace with final counsel-reviewed text before commercial launch.

## 1. Scope

This DPA applies when ShipDesk processes personal data on behalf of the Customer under the Terms of Service.

## 2. Roles

- **Customer** = data controller
- **ShipDesk** = data processor

## 3. Processing terms

- Purpose: providing shipping operations software
- Duration: for the term of the subscription
- Data subjects: Customer's end users, staff, and shipping recipients
- Categories of data: names, addresses, email, phone, shipment metadata

## 4. Sub-processors

Listed in our Privacy Policy. Customer is notified of additions via email with 30 days' notice.

## 5. Security measures

- TLS in transit
- Encryption at rest for sensitive fields
- Access controls with least-privilege principle
- Quarterly access audits

## 6. International transfers

Data stays in US-region AWS infrastructure at launch. EU region on the roadmap.

## 7. Audit rights

Once per 12-month period, with 30 days' notice, during business hours, at Customer's cost.

## 8. Data subject requests

ShipDesk provides self-service export and delete tools. For unusual requests, contact privacy@shipdesk.local.

## 9. Breach notification

Customer notified within 72 hours of a confirmed personal-data breach.

## 10. Termination

On termination, Customer's data is returned (export) or deleted within 30 days.
