# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 1, 2026

This is a placeholder. Replace before launch with final legal text reviewed by counsel.

## What we collect

- **Account info:** name, email, role, team membership
- **Shipping data:** addresses, parcels, shipment events, tracker events
- **Billing info:** handled by Stripe; we never store card numbers
- **Operational logs:** IP, user agent, audit trail of significant actions

## How we use it

Only to operate ShipDesk for you. We do not sell your data. We do not share with advertisers.

## Sub-processors

- **EasyPost** — label purchase and carrier routing
- **Stripe** — billing
- **AWS** — infrastructure (US region)
- **Sentry** — error tracking

## Your rights

- Access, export, and delete your data at any time (per-team export/delete)
- GDPR and CCPA compliant
- Contact privacy@shipdesk.local for requests

## Data retention

- Shipment records: 7 years (required by some carriers for audit)
- Audit logs: 2 years
- Deleted team data: purged within 30 days

## Security

- HTTPS only
- Encrypted at rest for sensitive fields (carrier credentials)
- Two-factor authentication available
- Tenant data isolation enforced at the database layer
